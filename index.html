Axum: A Journey Through Ethiopia's Past

Tigray Region, Ethiopia

Discover the ancient kingdom of Aksum, home to towering obelisks, the Queen of Sheba's palace, and legendary tales of the Ark of the Covenant, offering a journey through Ethiopia's fascinating historical and spiritual roots.

Lalibela: A Living Testament to Faith

Amhara Region, Ethiopia

Experience Lalibela, Ethiopia's "New Jerusalem," famed for its rock-hewn churches carved into the earth, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that enchants with its spiritual serenity and architectural marvels steeped in centuries-old tradition.

Erta Ale: The Ever-Burning Volcano

Danakil Depression, Afar Region, Ethiopia

Erta Ale, an active shield volcano in Ethiopia's Afar Region, entices adventurers. With a harsh climate, it reveals a lunar-like landscape. Trek to witness the surreal, simmering lava lake within the caldera, offering a unique and mesmerizing natural spectacle.

Fasilides: The Jewel of Gondar's History

Gondar, Amhara Region, Ethiopia

Step into history at Gondar's magnificent castle complex, known as Africa's Camelot, where majestic stone structures and royal tales from Ethiopia's 17th-century empire await amidst breathtaking highland landscapes.

Blue Nile: Ethiopia's Majestic River of Life

Amhara Region, Ethiopia

The Blue Nile Falls, located near Bahir Dar in Ethiopia, showcases the mighty Blue Nile River's descent. Surrounded by lush landscapes, the falls are particularly spectacular during the rainy season. Witness the impressive cascade and experience the natural beauty of the region, making it a captivating destination for nature lovers.

Explore the Simien Mountains' Rugged Beauty

Amhara Region, Ethiopia

The Simien Mountains, a UNESCO-listed site in northern Ethiopia, captivate with dramatic cliffs, deep valleys, and vibrant biodiversity. Featuring a temperate climate, it's a haven for hikers seeking encounters with endemic species like the Gelada baboon. This stunning landscape, characterized by its unique topography, offers a breathtaking escape into nature, making the Simien Mountains an extraordinary destination for those in search of both adventure and natural beauty.

Adwa Victory

Date: March 2, 2025
Venue: Across Ethiopia and Addis Ababa

The Battle of Adwa stands as a pivotal moment in Ethiopian and African history, where Ethiopian forces decisively defeated the invading Italian army in Adwa town, in northern Ethiopia. Led by Emperor Menelik II and Empress Taytu, the victory became a symbol of resistance against colonialism, inspiring liberation movements across Africa and beyond. Menilek II Square and the Adwa Victory Memorial are key venues for the celebration.

2025 Women First 5km Race

Date: Sunday, 16 March 2025
Time: Early Morning
Location: Bole District, Addis Ababa (Start/Finish near Atlas Hotel)

The Women First 5km Race celebrates women’s empowerment and fitness as part of the International Women’s Day (8 March) celebration. The race entry fee is £30 and includes an official race t-shirt and a souvenir medal.

Addis Chamber International Agriculture & Food Trade Fair

Date: February 13-15, 2025
Venue: Millennium Hall, Addis Ababa

15th Addis Chamber International Agriculture & Food Trade Fair (AGRIFLEX 2025) will take place in Addis Ababa from February 13th to 15th, 2025. This trade fair serves as a platform for companies involved in food, beverages, agriculture, farming, equipment, technologies, and packaging to showcase their products and services. It also facilitates collaboration between local and international businesses within the sector.

African Union Summit

Date: 15-16 February, 2025
Venue: AU Headquarters, Addis Ababa

The African Union (AU) Summit will be held at the African Union Headquarters in Addis Ababa by 15-16 February 2025. The summit serves as a platform for African leaders to deliberate on and address key challenges facing the continent, such as peace and security, economic growth, social integration, climate change, public health, and human rights. For more information, please visit : https://au.int
